    Frequently asked question

    Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

    • How can I track my order?

      Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can use this tracking number to track the progress of your shipment online.

    • What is delivery time of my order?

      Delivery time depends on your location and shipping method. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 1-3 business days.

    • How can I return my product?

      You can return your product within 30 days of purchase. Ensure the item is in its original condition and packaging. Visit our Returns page to initiate a return request.

    • What are the shipping costs for my order?

      Shipping costs vary based on your location and selected shipping method. Standard shipping is free for orders over $50, while express shipping starts at $9.99.

    • Can I chance or cancel my order after placing it?

      Once an order is placed, changes or cancellations are only possible within the first 2 hours. Please contact our customer support immediately for assistance.
